Magic Amongst Us

Synopsis

Born with the gift of magic Joleen always felt like she did not belong. She felt alone in a world where magic only seems possible in books or on tv.
One day her life took an unexpected turn when Clay appeared out of nowhere telling her she was not what she thoght was.
Crazy, right? But not as crazy as the adventures she got herself into when she agreed to listen to his story and meet his brother.
It all went downhill from there on. By the Gods she should have just stayed at home.

Conversation between Joleen and Clay

×

"Before we start I want to ask you something. Do you want to steal my magic?"
"And I have to tell you honestly I have no idea what I am up against nor do I have any experience with healing someone, well other than a little bird who broke its wing." Joleen said.
"So how is the bird doing?"
"I healed its wing... but it kind of exploded after that. You have to understand it was my first attempt at healing someone and I pushed too much of my magic on it. I've been having troubles controlling my magic latley."
"Yeah, well I am not in a rush to die so if you don't mind I would rather take my chances with normal medicine and hope for help from my Queen."

Conversation between Joleen, Oberon and Clay

×

"And you call yourself a man, little cry baby, its just a scratch"
Both Oberon and Clay had to laugh when she said that.
"She is right little brother, you can hardly see it."
"That is because I heal quickly. My arm was hanging on a few pieces of skin just moments ago, you guys just missed it."
That made Joleen and Oberon laugh so hard they had tears running down their faces.
"You do remember the time we had the discussion about being a man right? You were making fun of the way I ate my melon and calling me less of a man for it..."
Joleen stopped her laughing and leaned in to listen to this story because she had a feeling it would be a good one.
Clay Smiled. "Yes I do and I still stand by claim. A man that doesn't like his face getting wet while eating a melon off the peel is not a man at all. How can you even satisfy a woman if you can't even do that?"
"Well at least I have a woman to satisfy no matter how I eat my melon, you with your cry baby ways will never even get to that stage in the first place."
Clay smacked Oberon on his arm after his last remark and Oberon reacted by digging his finger into Clay's wound. That is when they started to fight in a way only brothers can. Joleen has seen them do this a few times before and now just let them be, still smiling about the conversation these two just had. Melons, really...

"Wow that is kind of high. How quickly do your bones heal?" Joleen walked up to him and glanced outside the window.

Interview with the characters

Joleen Abberdeen Therouchious Marlock Claytire Tripp

Joleen Abberdeen

×

Can you tell us a little bit about yourself?

“My name is Joleen Abberdeen and I am 23 years old. I come from a little town called Radiant and use to live there with just my dad before he passed. I worked as a server in a bar and can do a bit more than the average mortal or Fae. What? Well that you have to read for yourself.”

What would be your dream Crew of actors to play your story in the movies?

“I would love to choose actors that are starting ones because we all know what a hard life it is as a beginning artist. For Oberon I would choose a man that looked like a Jason Momoa type. For Clay I would say Golem but he is not a real character unfortunately, so maybe we could find an Ogre that would be crazy enough to play him. The resemblance would be striking. But all jokes aside someone a bit younger than the Oberon actor but with a good set of muscles and humor. For Eladrin I would choose that actor from the serie ‘Black sails’ I think he is called zach mcGowan, yeah him or a look a like just like that. Rough but handsome. For Morgana she has to be played by the most gorgeous looking woman that has yet to be discovered. All natural beauty. For the rest I have no idea, Others will have to think about it.”

Do you have a fun fact for us from the Magic amongst us story?

“Well funny thing is that I learned that a real man eats melon from the peel and not cut it up first. What that means well I can not tell you that without revealing something in the story, sorry guys!”

What profession would you be if you could choose anything?

“A preschool teacher. To work every day with little kids and help them smile and get stronger must be amazing. You see their development every day and I am amazed at how much they would grow both inside and out throughout the years. It must be amazing to be part of that growth”

What was the biggest struggle throughout your story in this book?

“Pfff, you are not making this easy on us are you. I will try to answer without revealing to much. My biggest struggle was to accept who I was and have always been. That is still part of my struggle today but I learned that I, like everyone else, matter no matter who you are or what you love or what you can or can not do. It's a long process but I do believe I have already come far from the Joleen in the beginning of the story to the Joleen that sits here with you today.”

My final question. For the people who can’t see you right now you are obviously pregnant. Congratulations first off and can I ask how far along you are and who the father is or is that also to revealing?

“Aww thank you, yeah it's pretty obvious isn’t it. I am due in 2 weeks if all goes to plan and the father is...Well you have to read about that in the book, I am sorry.”

I want to thank you for this interview and would love to ask you more in the future if you do not mind. Maybe do a fan Q&A? Also keep us updated with the birth please.

“That would be pretty cool. Just ask and I will answer as soon as I can. And when the baby arrives I will be sure to send you guys a card.”

Therouchious Marlock Claytire Tripp

×

Can you tell us a little bit about yourself?

“My name is Clay and I am the youngest of my family. I grew up with my older brother Oberon and 3 sisters, so it was a full house so to say. Me and my brother where placed at court as Sidhe because we come from a royal line, even if its very much a deluded nowadays, and serve our Queen as her private guards. I love hunting, mostly women and training with the other Sidhe. That’s about it I think.”

What are readers going to think about after they turn the last page of this book?

“That one is easy, me of course! I can’t reveal to much but I think I will conquer some hearts in the end and leave a lasting impression.”

So you are the most important thing in the whole story?

“Well no I wouldn’t put it like that. But without me the story would feel empty. But thinking about your question the readers might actually smile when they come to the end of this book and know not all is lost.

Is there going to be a big plot twist or surprise in this book you play a part in?

“You are asking hard questions that I can not answer without revealing some details of the story. But I do know that the story was written in a way to keep you involved from the first part to the last page. Maybe even longer.”

What sort of person is going to like the character Clay in this book?

“Well it is me not a character don’t forget, but to answer your question I think someone who likes jokes and doesn’t take everything too seriously is going to like me throughout the story. I love to joke around but that will make people love or hate you in the end.”

Who do you look up to?

“My brother Oberon and Joleen too. And do not forget my Queen Morgana Le Fay. They are all mountains in my life that I can always climb up to and hold on to when a storm hits. I seriously wouldn’t know what to do if I didn’t have these people around me.”

If you had a magic wand, what story would you love to be in?

“Fifty shades of course, but I would love to be that Christian guy with some more balls and a little more imagination. I mean why have a red room if you do not use it all the time with many, many women to please.”

Last question, If this story was to be turned into a movie, who would you like to play as you?

Chai Hansen, the guy that played the monkey king. He can fight like me and looks like my twin brother so that would be an easy pick.

I want to thank you for this interview and would love to ask you more in the future if you do not mind. Maybe do a fan Q&A?

“That would be awesome, count me in!”
