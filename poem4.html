Poems

R.H. Hammer

Missing

For the ones that have gone away
That didn’t get to always stay
The cuddles they will never give
The everlasting words of IF
If I just hadn’t let you go
Would your life have ended, though?
If I had never said those things
With the pains it sometimes brings
Would we have had another day
Or would you still have flown away
Was it all just meant to be
It’s tearing up inside of me
I wish we had just one more day
So you could hear these words I say
I might never hold you in my hands but forever in my thoughts
Forever there until my dying day, forever in our hearts
