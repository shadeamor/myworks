R.H. Hammer

Sight

Help me see the world ahead
When my eyes are closed
And help me awake from dire tread
When I shall need it most

Show me colours of the sky
For colour blind I’ll be
The shades, the forms, might scare at days
But you shall see set me free

Help me see the rain the falls
It’s like the salty sea
The roaring laugh of waves that break
Or clouds that come to me

Help me see the world ahead
Even when I can’t
Even when my eyes are dead
And buried in the sand

Help me hear the sounds of love
An everlasting beat
Make me forget the hate
Let it crumble at our feet

Help me stop the fear outside
The ever judging eye
Shield me in your warm embrace
And let the worries fly
