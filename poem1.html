Why I write

I write because it’s a way to talk
About the tender things
I write because it’s a way to walk
Through memories that ling’s
I write because my mind is full
Of adventures I wish I had
I write because it’s never dull
Even though it’s sometimes sad
I write to make someone smile one day
Make a darkened day shine bright
To walk alongside a readers way
And holding up their light
But mostly I write to tell them all
I have words to speak out loud
And even if my books might fall
They will always make me proud
