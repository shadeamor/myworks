R.H. Hammer

Reapers

Synopsis

Born with the mark given by a Reaper she never knew she had a destiny outside her own Realm until Mordecai showed up. He brought her to the In Between to be part of the I.B.R.D. (In Between Reaper Department) and maintain the balance between good and evil and keep the restless souls in check. It sucked living there where your neighbours are the ones you killed off. The looks alone will make you feel unwelcome. But the worst part of her new job as a Reaper wasn't the 'keeping her neighbours in check' part, it was actually collecting the souls of the innocent. But the ferryman waits for no one right?
