R.H. Hammer

How to describe my friendship?

My friendship sticks like Spiderman
I will never let you fall
I might hit you with Thor’s hammer
But I was just aiming at the wall
If ever I see green
Don’t worry no need to run
I am not turning into the Hulk
If there is nothing you have done
And if you ever need me
I will be there in a flash
Just give me a Bat signal
And I will quickly take a dash
No 3 time calls like Beetlejuice
To make me come and see
Fighting against all your demons
Even if Freddy Krueger it will be
Jokes I make like Deadpool
They don’t always come out right
But damn I am real funny
Even when I start a fight
So let us join the Justice League
Become a friendship group
But don’t you touch my Aquaman
Or I’ll make you into soup!
That there is my friendship
I offer all my friends
They will become my kryptonite
Until our story ends
